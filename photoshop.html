This is a logo I designed on Illustrator. For my concept, I wanted to do a logo that represented my favorite art medium: clay. I decided to incorporate my middle name, Jazmine. I chose brown and green to give it a natural, grounded feel because clay is an element of nature. Additionally, green can represent plants, which is why I added jasmine flowers to connect with the name Jazmine. The icon/symbol is a vase with jasmine flowers. I wanted to focus on organic shapes to mimic clay's freeform. I really like how for both designs, there aren't a lot of sharp edges.
