Nostalgic Connections

This virtual space was made as I thought about the connection I have with my younger siblings. There is a decade to two decades age gap between my five younger siblings and I. Sometimes, I wish I was able to grow up together with them. There are so many differences but also similarities in our childhoods. So, I decided to focus on how media, from cartoons/movies, to music, to video games mirror each other as decades go on.
