Welcome to my Net Art exploration! This project celebrates web-based artistic expression and internet-native creativity that exists only in the digital realm.

Net art is a form of artistic practice that uses the internet itself as both canvas and medium. It embraces hypertext, interactivity, networks, and the unique characteristics of web technologies to create works that couldn't exist anywhere else.

My net art projects explore themes of digital identity, online culture, connectivity, and the democratic nature of internet expression. Each piece takes advantage of the web's unique properties‚Äîits interactivity, hypertextuality, and global reach‚Äîto create meaningful artistic experiences.

It's art that lives online and invites participation from viewers around the world.
